J.K. Rowling - Author

J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for the Harry Potter series of books.

Biography

J.K. Rowling was born in Yate, Gloucestershire, England in 1965. She graduated from the University of Exeter with a degree in French and Classics. After working for Amnesty International and as a bilingual secretary, she began writing the Harry Potter series, which has sold over 500 million copies worldwide.

Works

  • Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
  • Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  • Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  • Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  • Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix
  • Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
  • Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows
