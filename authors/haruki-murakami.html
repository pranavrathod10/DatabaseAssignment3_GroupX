Haruki Murakami - Author

Haruki Murakami is a Japanese author best known for his novels and short stories.

Biography

Haruki Murakami was born in Kyoto, Japan in 1949. He attended Waseda University in Tokyo and opened a jazz bar after graduation. He began writing fiction in his spare time and published his first novel, Hear the Wind Sing, in 1979. His other notable works include Norwegian Wood, Kafka on the Shore, and 1Q84. He has won numerous awards for his writing, including the Franz Kafka Prize and the Jerusalem Prize.
