Stephen King - Author

Stephen King is an American author known for his horror, suspense, and supernatural fiction.

Biography

Stephen King was born in Portland, Maine in 1947. He graduated from the University of Maine with a degree in English. He began writing horror fiction in the early 1970s and has since published over 60 books, many of which have been adapted into movies or TV series.

  • The Shining
  • The Stand
