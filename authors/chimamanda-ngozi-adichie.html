Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie - Author

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie is a Nigerian author best known for her novels and essays exploring themes of feminism, race, and identity.

Biography

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie was born in Enugu, Nigeria in 1977. She studied medicine and pharmacy at the University of Nigeria before moving to the United States to attend Eastern Connecticut State University and then Johns Hopkins University, where she received a Master of Arts in creative writing. She is the author of several novels and essays, including Purple Hibiscus, Half of a Yellow Sun, and Americanah.
