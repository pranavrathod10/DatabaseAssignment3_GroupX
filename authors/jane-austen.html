Jane Austen - Author

Jane Austen was an English novelist known for her social commentary and romantic fiction.

Biography

Jane Austen was born in Steventon, Hampshire, England in 1775. She began writing at a young age and published her first novel, Sense and Sensibility, in 1811. Her other notable works include Pride and Prejudice, Emma, and Persuasion. Austen's writing often explores themes of love, marriage, and social class.
