Margaret Atwood - Author

Margaret Atwood is a Canadian author, poet, and literary critic.

Biography

Margaret Atwood was born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada in 1939. She studied at the University of Toronto and Radcliffe College, and has taught at various universities in Canada and the United States. She is the author of over 40 books, including The Handmaid's Tale and The Testaments.

Works

  • The Handmaid's Tale
  • The Testaments
  • Oryx and Crake
  • The Blind Assassin
  • The Year of the Flood
  • MaddAddam
